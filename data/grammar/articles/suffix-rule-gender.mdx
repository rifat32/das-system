# The Suffix Rule: Predicting Gender

**Don't memorize genders. Look at the ending.**

Most German nouns follow predictable patterns. By learning suffix rules, you can predict the gender of 80%+ of German nouns without memorization.

## The -ung Rule (Feminine - DIE)

One of the most reliable patterns in German: **Nouns ending in -ung are ALWAYS feminine (die).**
